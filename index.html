About Us

ChordConnect provides high-quality one-to-one online guitar lessons for learners of all ages and skill levels. We are a group of professional guitarists, each with our own distinct musical style. "Rock, Jazz and Blues" are our most popular classes. All newcomers receive FREE 50-minute lessons to give you a taste of the programme! Your tutor will then recommend the appropriate level for you - Beginner, Intermediate or Advanced. So, what are you waiting for? Let the music take control and jam with us!
