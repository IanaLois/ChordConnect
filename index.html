About us

ChordConnect offers high-rated one to one online guitar lessons for all ages and all skill levels.
We're a team of professional guitarists, each specialising their own unique music style.
Our most popular classes are "Rock, Jazz and Blues".

All First-time ChordConnectors are given 2 FREE 30min lessons!
So you can try out our programme and we'll recommend the level that best suits you.

Now, let the music take control and jam with us!
