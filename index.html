About us

ChordConnect offers high-rated one to one online guitar lessons for all ages and all skill levels.

We're a team of professional guitarists, each specialising their own unique music style - Our most popular picks are "Rock, Jazz and Blues".

All First-time ChordConnectors are given 2 FREE 30min lessons to try out the program and to determine which level best suits you.

So, Let the music take control and jam with us!
